Wines from Swiss Chalet Inn, Okanagan Valley

The Swiss Chalet Inn is a family-owned holiday resort & health and wellness retreat that has recently branched out on a seasonal basis to sell its own wine produced in local vineyards of the Okanagan Valley, BC, Canada. Our vines come in a wide range of notes. We sell our wines online and orders can be delivered to customers based in Canada and the US.

Some of the wine varieties that come out of the Valley are Chardonnay, Viognier, Cabernet Sauvignon, Syrah, locally produced versions of Portuguese Terra Boa. We sell our products online for the rest of year to make up for the low revenue during the long winter months. We are deeply grateful for the support and loyalty from our clients who have been regularly visiting our resort us for years.
